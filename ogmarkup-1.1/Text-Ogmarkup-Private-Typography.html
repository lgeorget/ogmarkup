Inner spaces representation

data Space

Deal with typographic spaces, especially when it comes to separate two texts. Because Space derives Ord, it is possible to use min and max to determine which one to use in case of a conflict.

Constructors

Normal

A normal space that can be turned into a newline for displaying.

Nbsp

A non breakable space, it cannot be turned into a newline.

None

No space at all.

data Typography a

A Typography is a data type that tells the caller what space she should privileged before and after a text.
